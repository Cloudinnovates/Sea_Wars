What is Sea Wars?

Basic Info

Sea Wars - is a simple 2 player turn based game well known in CIS region. The goal of this play is to kill enemy ships, but not lose own. The original game uses two 10x10 battlefields - allied and enemies. Each player has 10 ships: 1 x 4-deck, 2 x 3-deck, 3 x 2-deck and 4 x 1-deck. Each player shoot associated with letter plus number coordinate. Letter - stands for column of battlegrid and number - stands for row. In this online game variation of Sea Wars coordinates notation is not displayed because of no need. Online system takes all responsibility for shooting, result reporting proccesses. Thats make our game much more faster and dynamic (avarage duration 6-7 min).

Landing Ships

When all players are loaded game begins with landing stage. Each player should land his ships over the battlefield. Click on ship to select him and chose his position with a second click on your sea grid. You can switch vertical and horizontal ship orientation by clicking special rotate button on the top of your battlescreen. Sea Wars using standard well-known landing rules - any of landed ships should't "touch" to each other directly. The only differents with original game is that you can land two ships that is "touched" by their corner. That have been made intentionally diverse and interesting. After you land all of your ships the system will automatically give a message that your're ready to battle.

Battling State

The battling state begin when both players have landed their ships. The system will automatically chose which player will have a first turn for shooting. Turn info is displeyed by green/greed battlefield highlighting and "Sea Captain's" replic. If player misses his shoot the turn will go to his enemy, otherwise, if he hits, he's saving his turn and is able to continue shooting his enemy. When all ships of some player are dead, the game over message will appear, and system will ask user if he wants to save more detailed game results (with shooting history and statistics). Your recieve all info about your game on profile page by clicking your nickname or logo on navbar.

Who is Sea Captain?

Sea Captain is your game spectator and logger. He will comment all your moves and will give you some hints in the battle. Also he may help you to land a ships - just click on him while landing stage is going on and he will automatically finish all routine for you. If you want Sea Captain to tell some fun joke, type "/joke" in the chat, and he will tell some "Chuck Norris"-like joke about himself to both players.
